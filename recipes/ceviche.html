Ceviche

Description

A beautiful bowl of ceviche, which has lime and lemon juice, shrimp, tomatoes, cucumber, cilantro, avocado, onion, salt and pepper.

Ingredients

  • lime juice
  • lemon juice
  • shrimp
  • tomatoes
  • cucumber
  • cilantro
  • avocado
  • onion
  • salt
  • pepper

Steps

  1. cut all Ingredients and mix everything except the shrimp into a bowl
  2. cook the shrimp and add the shrimp to the bowl
  3. enjoy
